Why we started

Roshn Kal began as a small group of students who kept noticing the same gap: real need on one side, and willing hands on the other, with no bridge between them.

So we built one — a youth-run organization that turns that willingness into scholarships, shelter, and support, for people and animals alike. Every event we run and every rupee we raise goes back into closing that gap a little further.
