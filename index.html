Welcome to Roshn Kal

Roshn Kal is a student-led initiative dedicated to uplifting society by creating opportunities for individuals, especially those held back by their circumstances.

Get Involved Explore Initiatives

What we do

Serving people, and the animals beside them

We are dedicated to serving underprivileged communities and promoting animal welfare, working on the belief that a compassionate society looks after every member of it — human and animal alike.

Why choose us

Driven by youth, built for impact

Driven by youth, we bridge the gap for underprivileged communities and give a voice to animal welfare — combining energy with accountability in everything we run.

Our focus areas

Where we put our energy

For Underprivileged Communities

Improving access to education, healthcare, food, clothing and essential resources through scholarships, skill development and emergency support.

For Animal Welfare

Providing food, shelter and medical care to animals in need, protecting their well-being and the care they deserve.

Community Services

Sports events, MUNs, seminars and workshops that bring people together — with funds raised supporting our charitable projects.
